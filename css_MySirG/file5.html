About Our College

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel College Bhabua Kaimur, with more than six decades of rich history, stands as an epitome of knowledge and freedom in a place famous for beautiful natural surroundings and ancient cultural heritage sites of Maa Mundeshwari and Harsu Brahm[1].

We impart higher education to our students so that they become better human beings and make a positive impact in society[1]. Our team of excellent teachers and learning resources offer a comprehensive perspective to students through innovative pedagogy and value-added education[1].
