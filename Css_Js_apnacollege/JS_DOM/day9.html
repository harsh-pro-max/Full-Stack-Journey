Spider Man

About

Spider-Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics Created by writer=editor Stan Lee and aritist Steve Ditko,he first appeard in the anthology comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 (auguest 1962) in the Silver age of Comic books. He has been featured in comic books, television shows films video games, novels, and plays
